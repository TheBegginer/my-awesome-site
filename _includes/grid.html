The Internet and Me

The Internet has a way of teaching itself how it works. It is a realy big circle of knowledge rolling and growing. Being a part of that circle is the only way of having a peek of all that there is to known about the Internet

Going to college and learn about programing, how machines works, how they talk to each other, the way a systems interacts with other, was just the key in the keyhole, opening the gate to a bigger thing. The road beyond the gate I have traveled driven by curiosity and stubborness. Stubborness for working on things, until things worked the exact way I intended them to.

Source of knowledge

Knowledge can come from anywhere, I have had a lot of teachers in the Internet, some I name here others not. There have been so many and from many places, that is imposible to name them all, none the less I give thank to them all equally.

Sebastián Pérez
